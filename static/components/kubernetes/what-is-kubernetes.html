What is Kubernetes?

A platform & toolkit for developing distributed system/applications

This includes:

  • - Service discovery & Load-Balancing
  • - Distributed configuration management
  • - Resource allocation
  • - Container orchestration
  • - Canary Deployments
In short: Everything you need to build a highly distributed system.

One bad point I discovered: It highly depends on distributed file-storage systems.

Local Storage is, at the moment, in alpha. You have to activate it.
